About

I'm an Irish web developer, mediocre musician, and graduate of a rather odd degree called Music, Media and Performance Technology in University of Limerick. I never really worked out what that degree is meant to prepare me for, but I do know that I got to spend a lot of time doing cool things, and somehow had a job at the end of it. So I guess that's a success!

You can generally find me messing around with some half-finished project on my laptop, playing guitar/piano with friends, giving a hastily prepared speech at Toastmasters, or just chatting about nerdy stuff in 091 Labs (Galway's best and only hackerspace!).

In April 2013 I signed a pledge to donate at least 10% of my income to the most effective charities, and am a firm believer that if you're going to spend resources trying to make the world a better place, you may as well try and get the most bang for your buck! I don't talk about that the do-gooder stuff much in person, because I usually feel like a bit of an ass when I do. If you're interested though, I'm more than happy to chat!

This site is created in Angularjs, with a little JSON API hacked on to the front of the Anchor CMS blogging platform (which I was using to power the blog before I redid the frontend in Angular). I hope to replace that soon with a new backend in Ruby or Python.
